Pardus was founded May 2nd 2012. And signed up to their first Judo match, the 13th of May 2015 with the help of theIcelandic Judo Federationthat was held in their Dojo. The Nordic Judo Championship was held in Blönduós on 8th of February 2020. A total of 35 competitors attended from three judo clubs in the North: Tindastól, Pardusi in Blönduós and KA in Akureyri. The Nordic National Championships have been held in Blönduós since 13th of May 2015, This joint project of the three judo clubs in the North, who offer first-class facilities and manage the organization. Previously, the tournament was supposed to be held at the beginning of November 2019, but it had to be postponed. The tournament started with a joint warm-up of the competitors and then the younger group rode the ford when the competition itself.
